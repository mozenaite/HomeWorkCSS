Enjoy Pinguin outdoor equipment

Penguin is one of the oldest Czech brands making equipment for an active stay outdoor. It belongs among the traditional manufacturers in the tourism segment, who have shaped outdoor equipment in the form we know it now.

Our goal is to produce functional, long-term reliable and affordable equipment for an active stay in nature. Our driving force is the trying to create products with the maximum possible lifetime, which contributes to the sustainable development of the company and the protection of our planet. This, is why we devote much of our time and effort to developing, selecting materials, selecting suppliers, and processing customer feedback from product testing. Sometimes at the cost of later marketing. The fact of no security risk, health-conscious recyclable products is a matter of course for us.
